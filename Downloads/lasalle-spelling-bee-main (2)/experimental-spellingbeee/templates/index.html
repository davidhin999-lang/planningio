Official Ranked Rules

🏆 Competition Overview

  • Weekly Competition: New rankings every Monday
  • Attempts Per Week: 3 attempts per player
  • Best Score Only: Only your highest score counts
  • Team Competition: Your score adds to team total

⏱️ Timing System

  • Starting Time: 14 seconds per word
  • Time Reduction: -1 second every 2 correct words
  • Minimum Time: 4-6 seconds (progressive)
  • Anti-Snowball: Faster shrink for high performers

🎯 Scoring Breakdown

  • Base Points: 10 points per letter
  • Speed Bonus: Up to 30% of base points
  • Accuracy Bonus: Up to 20% of base points
  • Combo Bonus: 10% for speed + accuracy
  • Perfect Word: ×1.2 multiplier (no errors + fast)

💣 Special Words

  • Bomb Intervals: Words 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 35, 40
  • Bomb Bonus: ×2 multiplier on bomb word
  • Bomb Penalty: -10% total score if failed (min 10)

🔥 Streak Multipliers

  • 1-2 words: ×1.0 (no bonus)
  • 3-4 words: ×1.2
  • 5-7 words: ×1.4
  • 8-12 words: ×1.6
  • 13+ words: ×1.7 (maximum)

🛡️ Streak Protection

  • Streak Bank: Save points at milestones (3, 5, 8, 12)
  • Streak Shield: Protect streak once at 5+ streak
  • Recovery: Get up to 100 points back when streak breaks

⚠️ Anti-Cheating

  • Tab Switching: 3 switches = game invalidated
  • Attempt Guard: Attempt consumed after 3 correct words
  • Score Verification: All scores tracked for consistency

🎮 Strategy Tips

  • Balance Speed & Accuracy: Both bonuses stack
  • Maintain Streaks: Use shield strategically
  • Master Bomb Words: ×2 bonus is significant
  • Perfect Words: Aim for no errors + fast completion
